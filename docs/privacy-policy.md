# Privacy Policy for MonoTimer

**Last Updated: April 20, 2026**

## Introduction

MonoTimer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected via Sign in with Apple for authentication purposes
- **Apple ID**: Used for secure authentication (we do not store your Apple ID)

### Usage Data
- **Focus Sessions**: Start time, end time, duration, and session type (focus/break)
- **App Preferences**: Timer settings, daily goals, and notification preferences
- **Device Information**: iOS version, device type (for app functionality only)

### Analytics Data (Opt-In)
With your consent, we collect anonymous product analytics to improve the app. This includes:
- **Product Interaction Events**: Screens viewed, buttons tapped, and features used (e.g., "paywall_viewed", "timer_started")
- **Anonymous User Identifier**: A random, non-personal ID used to group events from the same device
- **App Session Lifecycle**: App launches, foreground/background events

We do **not** collect free-form text input, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information for analytics. Session replay is disabled in this release.

You can opt out at any time in **Settings → Privacy → Share Usage Data**. When opted out, no analytics events are sent from your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate your account via Sign in with Apple
- Sync your focus session data across your devices
- Provide personalized statistics and progress tracking
- Send local notifications for timer completion and daily habit reminders
- Understand feature usage to improve the app (anonymous analytics, with your consent)
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage**: Session data is stored securely on Supabase (a third-party service provider)
- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses industry-standard HTTPS encryption
- **Local Storage**: App preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Selling**: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: For authentication and data synchronization ([Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Apple Sign In**: For secure authentication ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))
- **PostHog**: For anonymous product analytics (opt-in only) ([PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy))
- **Apple App Store**: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data, either in-app (Profile → Delete Account) or by contacting us
- **Opt-Out of Analytics**: Disable anonymous analytics at any time in Settings → Privacy → Share Usage Data
- **Opt-Out of Sync**: Disable data syncing by signing out

To exercise these rights, contact us at: booya.pannachai@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

MonoTimer offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (MonoTimer Premium). All purchases are processed by **Apple** through the App Store. We do **not** receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Apple shares with us only:
- Whether your subscription is active, expired, or in a grace period
- The product identifier and period of your active subscription (e.g., monthly or yearly)
- An anonymous transaction identifier used to validate purchases

For information about how Apple handles your payment and billing data, see the [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time in your iOS Settings under **Apple ID → Subscriptions**.

## Children's Privacy

MonoTimer is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
